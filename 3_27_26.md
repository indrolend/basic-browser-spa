
# HomeDev Progress Summary — 3/27/26 (Updated)

- Refactored the SPA to use DOM-based hero rendering at rest (GIFs animate as <img>, text as <div>), improving accessibility and modularity.
- Added a dedicated transition canvas overlay for all hero transitions, ensuring separation of idle and animation states.
- Implemented a pixel-perfect particle transition effect:
  - Both the current and next hero are rasterized and cropped to their visible content (non-transparent bounding box).
  - The transition effect always aligns the visible content, even for long text or transparent GIFs.
  - The transition canvas is only visible during animation, ensuring seamless swaps.
- Navigation (section/item) and rendering logic is modular, minimal, and easy to follow.
- Section/item structure is fully defined and matches the asset folder layout.
- Keyboard and swipe navigation are implemented and working.
- Hero assets (GIFs from `/gifs` or fallback text) are loaded dynamically per item, with correct filename mapping.
- Styling is unified: dark theme, readable, and minimal.
- All navigation and transitions are tested for bugs and UX issues.
